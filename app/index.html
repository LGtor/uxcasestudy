It Starts When A Digital Problem Meets A Creative Brainstorm

Client

Beautezine.com

Problem:

The CEO had heard some readers explain that the homepage (dispite having regular new content) appeared as if it had content updates less-frequently do to the layout of the thumbnails on the home page. She also observed a frequent number of entrances to the site on the "recent" posts page, indicating that readers were bookmarking a page other the homepage and were often times bypassing new content.
